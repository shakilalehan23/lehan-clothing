Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is your delivery time?

We usually deliver within 3-5 working days across Sri Lanka. Colombo and suburbs might receive orders within 1-2 days.

2. Can I return or exchange an item?

Yes, you can exchange items within 7 days of delivery, provided they are unworn and have original tags attached. Please contact our support for more details.

3. Do you have a physical store?

Currently, we are an online-exclusive brand. However, you can check our sizing guide to ensure a perfect fit.

4. How can I track my order?

Once your order is shipped, you will receive a tracking number via email or SMS to monitor its progress.
