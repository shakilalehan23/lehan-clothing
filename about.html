Our Story.

Redefining streetwear in Sri Lanka.

The Beginning

Lehan Clothing was founded with the aim of providing the youth of Sri Lanka with quality and modern fashion at an affordable price. Every one of our creations is produced using the highest quality materials. From sketching on raw paper to delivering the final piece to your wardrobe, our journey is fueled by passion, art, and the love for uncompromised fashion.

Our Mission

Our main objective is to give you a unique look. We strictly follow minimalist design principles combined with aggressive streetwear aesthetics to stand out from the crowd. We promise comfort without sacrificing the luxury feel.
