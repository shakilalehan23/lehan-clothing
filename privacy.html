Privacy Policy

At Lehan Clothing, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This policy outlines how we handle your information.

1. Data Collection

We collect personal information such as name, email address, phone number, and delivery address when you make a purchase or subscribe to our newsletter.

2. Usage of Data

The information we collect is used solely to process orders, improve our services, and communicate with you about your order or our latest collections.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to outside parties except for the specific purpose of delivering the purchased product.

4. Cookies

Our website uses cookies to enhance your browsing experience and analyze site traffic for better service delivery.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information held by us at any time. Simply contact our support for assistance.
